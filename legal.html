Corporate Governance & Integrity Policy

Effective Date: 3rd June, 2024

AR Holdings Group is committed to the highest standards of ethics, transparency, and compliance with applicable laws in all jurisdictions in which it operates.

The Company maintains:

  • Zero tolerance for fraud, harassment, extortion, or threats.
  • Immediate escalation of credible allegations of misconduct to internal legal counsel and external authorities.
  • Independent verification of any claims affecting the Company's reputation or operations.

All employees, contractors, and representatives are bound by contractual and legal obligations to protect the Company's reputation, confidential information, and business relationships.

Violation of these obligations may result in termination, legal proceedings, and reporting to relevant regulatory or law enforcement bodies.
