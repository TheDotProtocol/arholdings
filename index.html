In Loving Memory

AK

1987 - 2025

It is with deep sadness that we announce the passing of our beloved founder, AK.

AK was the visionary founder of AR Holdings, whose strategic foresight and entrepreneurial spirit laid the foundation for a multi-industry conglomerate. Under his guidance, AR Holdings became a pioneer in blockchain technology, fintech innovation, hospitality, logistics, and FMCG.

His leadership was defined by relentless pursuit of excellence, commitment to empowering others, and a visionary approach to technology and business. His legacy continues to inspire the company's culture, innovation, and strategy.

During this difficult time, AR Holdings is undergoing a period of restructuring to ensure the continued success and stability of our organization. We remain committed to maintaining the high standards and values that our founder established while preparing for the next chapter in our company's journey.

As we honor our founder's legacy, we reaffirm our commitment to his vision of innovation and excellence. Our leadership team remains fully engaged to ensure seamless operations, and we look forward to continuing to build on the foundation he created.
